Temizleyiciler

After a certain period of service, styrene or mold release waxes start to build up on the mold surface. Cleaning this debris is a delicate job as you do not want to use strong solvents and damage the mold surface while cleaning it. SupraClean mold cleaner series are designed to clean the molds thoroughly while avoiding any damage on the mold surface.
