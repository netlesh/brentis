Mold Cleaner C150

Brentis mold cleaner C150 is a solvent mixture that is used for cleaning the FRP molds easily and quickly. If dissolves accumulated waxes , resin and polystyrene debris off the mold surface.


Mold Cleaner A100

Brentis mold cleaner A100 is a mixture of surfactants and non-agressive solvent which cleans the mold thoroughly without damaging the mold surface. It is especially effective for cleaning semi-permanent and silicone based release agents. Apply a small amount on the mold surface and wash with water.
