Parea

7416 BL
Polyaspartic Polyurea Floor Coating Systems

Parea 7416BL is a high performance coating material that can easily be applied by a roller.

It is used in all coating applications where there is a need for durability and elegant look.

  • Tough and durable
  • Excellent resistance against mechanical forces, impact and UV exposure.
  • Can be cleaned easily
  • Provides absolute moisture and water proofing
  • Application is quick and easy
  • Can be applied at temperatures as low as 32°F
  • Back to service in 4 hours for light traffic
  • Full curing takes place in 24 hours

Let your imagination speak...

Parea 7416BL is a new generation polyaspartic polyurea resin that is presented by Brentis. It offers the users and applicators a variety of design possibilities.
Simply roll it on concrete, wood or glass to create a classy looking place you have always dreamed of. It is available in various colors and it can also be mixed with decorative flakes, chips or sand.

Due to its aliphatic chemistry, Parea 7416BL is highly resistant against outdoor conditions and UV exposure. It does not fade or yellow over time.

When cured properly, Parea 7416BL forms a very elastic film with excellent adhesion on the substrate. Therefore it does not crack, chip or peel off like similar floor coating materials do.

While forming a flexible, tough and high performance film on the substrate, Parea 7416BL also offers flexibility to the user. You can apply it on different substrates and create lots of different combinations.

It contains a proprietary chemistry that bonds organic materials to inorganic materials.
